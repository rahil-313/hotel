Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I make a booking? To book a hotel, check-in and check-out dates, and the number of guests on our homepage. Then browse the available options and follow the prompts to complete your reservation.

2. Can I modify or cancel my reservation? Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation through your account or by contacting our customer support team. Please check our cancellation policy for specific details.

3. What payment methods do you accept? We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other payment methods. You can select your preferred option at checkout.
